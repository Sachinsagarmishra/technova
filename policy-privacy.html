This Privacy Policy explains how TechNova Systems Inc. (“TechNova,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, discloses, and protects personal information when you visit our website, contact us, submit a resume, apply for an opportunity, request staffing or consulting services, or otherwise interact with us.

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide

Depending on how you use our website and services, you may provide:

  • Contact details such as your name, email address, telephone number, company, job title, and location.
  • Candidate and career information such as your resume, employment history, education, skills, certifications, compensation expectations, work authorization, preferred role, portfolio, LinkedIn profile, and availability.
  • Employer and business inquiry information, including hiring needs, project requirements, timelines, team size, and messages submitted through our forms.
  • Communications and any other information you choose to send to us.

Information collected automatically

When you use our website, we and our service providers may automatically collect technical and usage information, such as IP address, browser type, device information, referring pages, pages viewed, approximate location derived from IP address, dates and times of visits, and interactions with the website. We may collect this information through cookies and similar technologies as described in our Cookie Policy.

Information from other sources

We may receive professional or business information from recruiting platforms, professional networks, job boards, referrals, clients, public sources, and service providers where permitted by law.

2. How We Use Information

We may use personal information to:

  • Respond to inquiries and provide staffing, recruiting, consulting, and talent-related services.
  • Evaluate candidate qualifications, match candidates with relevant opportunities, and communicate about roles or career resources.
  • Understand employer requirements and support recruiting, workforce, and technology engagements.
  • Operate, secure, troubleshoot, personalize, and improve our website and services.
  • Send service communications and, where permitted, relevant business or career updates. You may opt out of marketing communications at any time.
  • Prevent fraud, protect our rights and systems, comply with legal obligations, and enforce our terms.

3. How We Disclose Information

We may disclose information to:

  • Clients and prospective employers: Candidate information may be shared for relevant opportunities, consistent with the purpose for which it was provided and applicable law.
  • Service providers: Vendors that support hosting, communications, form processing, analytics, security, recruiting operations, and other business functions may process information on our behalf.
  • Professional advisers and authorities: We may disclose information when reasonably necessary to comply with law, respond to lawful requests, protect rights or safety, or establish and defend legal claims.
  • Business transactions: Information may be transferred in connection with a merger, financing, acquisition, reorganization, sale of assets, or similar transaction.

We do not knowingly sell personal information for monetary consideration. Certain analytics or advertising-related disclosures may be treated as “sharing” or a “sale” under some privacy laws; where applicable, you may exercise the rights described below.

4. Data Retention

We retain personal information for as long as reasonably necessary to provide services, maintain business and recruiting records, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. Retention periods vary based on the type of information, our relationship with you, and applicable law.

5. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect personal information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Privacy Choices and Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to request access, correction, deletion, or portability of personal information, or to object to or restrict certain processing. You may also have the right to opt out of certain targeted advertising, sales, or sharing, and to appeal a decision regarding a privacy request.

To submit a request, email Info@technovasystemsinc.com. We may need to verify your identity before completing a request. Authorized agents may submit requests where permitted by law. We will not discriminate against you for exercising applicable privacy rights.

7. International Data Transfers

TechNova and its service providers may process information in the United States and other countries where data protection laws may differ from those in your location. Where required, we use appropriate safeguards for international transfers.

8. Children’s Privacy

Our website and services are intended for business users and job candidates and are not directed to children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16 through this website.

9. Third-Party Links

Our website may link to third-party websites or services. Their privacy practices are governed by their own policies, and TechNova is not responsible for those practices.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date on this page shows when the policy was most recently revised. Material changes may be communicated through the website or other appropriate means.
