This Cookie Policy explains how TechNova Systems Inc. (“TechNova,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit our website. It should be read together with our Privacy Policy.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your browser or device when you visit a website. Similar technologies, including pixels, tags, local storage, and software development tools, may perform related functions. These technologies can help websites operate, remember choices, understand usage, and improve security and performance.

2. How We Use Cookies

TechNova and service providers supporting our website may use the following categories of cookies and similar technologies:

Strictly necessary cookies

These technologies support essential website functions, security, network management, form operation, fraud prevention, and user-requested services. Disabling them may prevent parts of the website from working properly.

Functional cookies

These technologies remember preferences and choices, support embedded features, and help provide a more consistent website experience.

Analytics and performance cookies

These technologies help us understand how visitors use the website, which pages are viewed, how users arrive at the website, and whether errors occur. We use this information to measure and improve website performance, content, and usability.

Advertising or social media technologies

Third-party links, embedded media, or social features may allow their providers to collect information about your interaction with those features. Depending on the provider and your settings, this information may be used to measure content or personalize experiences on other services.

3. Third-Party Technologies

Our website is built with WordPress and may use hosting, security, analytics, form-processing, embedded media, maps, and social media services. These providers may set or access their own cookies when their features are used. Their handling of information is governed by their respective privacy and cookie policies.

4. Your Cookie Choices

You can manage cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to view, block, or delete cookies and to control how websites store data. Blocking all cookies may affect website functionality, including forms, security features, and saved preferences.

Where a cookie preference or consent tool is displayed on the website, you may use it to manage available non-essential categories. You can also adjust privacy controls provided by third-party services or devices. Browser-based opt-out signals, such as Global Privacy Control, may be recognized where required by applicable law.

5. How Long Cookies Remain

Some cookies are deleted when you close your browser (“session cookies”), while others remain until they expire or are deleted (“persistent cookies”). Duration depends on the cookie’s purpose and the provider that sets it.
